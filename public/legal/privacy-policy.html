Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Sport Streak Academy collects only the data needed to provide training features, account access, streak tracking, and app reliability.

What We Collect

  • Account data (email, profile identifiers)
  • Training activity (drills completed, streak progress, challenge events)
  • Device and diagnostics data for crash/error monitoring
  • Optional analytics data if you grant consent

How We Use Data

  • Deliver and improve core app functionality
  • Maintain account security and authentication
  • Detect, prevent, and debug service issues
  • Analyze product usage only when consent is granted

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with infrastructure providers required to operate the service (for example hosting, database, and notification providers).

Retention

We retain data while your account is active and as needed for legal, security, and operational obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can decline analytics consent in-app.
  • You can request account deletion at any time (see Account Deletion page).
